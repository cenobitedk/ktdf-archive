Announcement #8: GRAVE MIASMA (uk)!!!

Announcement #8; a lot has happened since 2011 where the band we are about announce played KTDF; a full length, a MLP, tours in Europe, US, South America and Australia. These guys are legends on the international underground scene and need no further introduction. We are proud to announce that UK´s no. 1 occult death metal entity GRAVE MIASMA will join us for a cavernous sonic ritual at KTDF 2018!

Formed in London, England in 2002 under the name of Goat Molestör they released  their first demo tape, Ancient Barbaric Assault as well as the Realm Of Evoked Doom 7″ in 2005.

The band changed its name to Grave Miasma in 2006 and eventually released the Exalted Emanation MLP on September 9th 2009 on Sepulchral Voice Records. The Realm Of Evoked Doom EP was later re-released on CD with improved sound by Nuclear Winter Records.

After a small break and a handful of gigs and tours in Europe and the USA, Grave Miasma released their debut full-length album entitled Odori Sepulcrorum on 13.9.13. This was released by Sepulchral Voice Records in Europe and Profound Lore Records in the USA. The band embarked on a 14-date European tour with Necros Christos shortly after.

In 2016 Grave Miasma released the “Endless Pilgrimage” MLP following up their “Odori Sepulcrorum” milestone album by taking their sound further into darker, more atmospheric and more progressive territory channeling some of their most challenging, aggressive, and most advanced material to date. With “Endless Pilgrimage”, Grave Miasma have harnessed a new level of creativity to their oeuvre creating another unparalleled work within the genre.
