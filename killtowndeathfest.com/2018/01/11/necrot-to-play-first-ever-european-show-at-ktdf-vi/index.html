Necrot to play first ever European show at KTDF VI!

We are back with our second announcement; tonight we are proud to announce that Bay Area crushers NECROT will do their first ever European performance at KTDF – The Resurrection!

The last year has been active for NECROT after the release of their critically acclaimed debut album “Blood Offerings” in the summer of 2017. They toured for more than 2 months around the US and Canada after the release promoting the album and after cancelling their European tour in October 2017, we’re excited that NECROT will spill their first ever Blood Offerings at KTDF 2018!

Back in 2011 Luca (bass & vocals) and Chad (drums) started out recording and releasing their first two demos only consisting of the two as members, as they couldn’t find the right person to join the band on guitar to fit with what they had in mind. After having Luca play guitars on the recordings, they found local guitar-swinger Sonny to form the steady line-up to play and record their third and final demo “The Labyrinth” showed what was to come. Having always been busy keeping their presence visible in their local scene and both coasts of America, 2017 saw the release of their debut album “Blood Offerings” and first full US tour over the summer. The album gathered them a lot of well-deserved attention and soon after they were offered yet another big US tour supporting Suffocation forcing them to cancel their planned European tour that year. That will however now be changed as we have the honor of showing NECROT for the first time in Europe at KTDF 2018.
